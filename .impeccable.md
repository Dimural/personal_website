## Design Context

### Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, and fellow engineers landing on Dimural Murat's
personal portfolio — usually on desktop, sometimes mobile, often skimming for
"is this person good?" in under a minute. The job to be done: convey craft and
taste quickly, then make it trivial to reach out or explore work.

### Brand Personality
Three words: **engineered, tactile, considered.** Quietly confident, not loud.
The site itself is the proof of work — every detail is intentional. Should evoke
calm focus and a sense of "this person sweats the details," not hype.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark, minimal, editorial. Near-black green-tinted canvas with a single ambient
Aurora glow behind the hero name. Generous negative space, restrained motion,
one luminous green accent used sparingly. Anti-references: cluttered "kitchen
sink" of competing WebGL effects, neon cyan/purple AI gradients, glassmorphism
everywhere, centered everything.

### Type System
- **Display:** Zodiak (Fontshare) — high-contrast modern serif with character,
  for the name and section titles. (Instrument Serif deliberately avoided.)
- **Body / UI:** Geist — clean engineered sans for prose, nav, buttons.
- **Mono accent:** Geist Mono — ONLY for the tiny numbered section index labels
  (e.g. `01 — ABOUT`). Never for body text.

### Palette (OKLCH, neutrals tinted toward brand green ~165h)
- `--bg`        oklch(0.165 0.008 165)  near-black green
- `--surface`   oklch(0.205 0.010 165)  lifted panel
- `--text`      oklch(0.950 0.006 150)  off-white
- `--text-dim`  oklch(0.720 0.012 160)  secondary
- `--text-faint`oklch(0.540 0.012 162)  tertiary / labels
- `--border`    oklch(0.300 0.012 165)
- `--accent`    oklch(0.800 0.130 158)  luminous green (rare, 10%)
- Aurora stops: green-forward `#2f6f54 → #3c7d74 → #557a9e` (muted, not neon)

### Design Principles
1. **One signature, everything else quiet.** Aurora is the only ambient effect;
   CardSwap is the one interactive focal moment. Nothing else competes.
2. **Space is the design.** Lean on rhythm and negative space over chrome.
3. **Green is rare.** The accent earns its power by appearing seldom.
4. **Left-aligned, asymmetric.** Avoid centering everything; let layouts breathe.
5. **Restraint in motion.** Smooth ease-out reveals, no bounce/elastic.
