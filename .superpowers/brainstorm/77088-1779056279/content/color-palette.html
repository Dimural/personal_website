Color Direction for the Voxel Gym

Your site uses whites, light grays, and blue (#0071e3). How should the gym adapt that into a Crossy Road voxel style?

A — Light & Clean

Light wood floors, soft gray equipment. Blue accent pops on key interactive pieces. Warm/airy. Closest to the main site's whitespace feel.

B — Dark & Electric

Dark charcoal floor with blue glow on equipment. High-contrast, moody gym atmosphere. More dramatic and game-like.

C — Warm Wood

Warm oak wood floors, terracotta equipment tones, blue accents. Athletic club aesthetic. Rich and earthy, still clean.
