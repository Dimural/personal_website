What color tone for the luminous accents?

The ShaderGradient sphere and subtle highlights will use one of these tones throughout the site. Click to choose.

Arctic Blue

Crisp, cool blue-to-white gradient. Clean and professional. The classic "tech" palette — but done with restraint it feels refined rather than generic.

Rose Silk

Warm rose-to-blush gradient. More personal and human. Stands out strongly in the tech-portfolio space — sophisticated without being loud.

Aurora

Shifting lavender-to-cyan gradient — the closest to what ShaderGradient does naturally. Ethereal, unexpected, and extremely aesthetic. Feels like light through crystal.
